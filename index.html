🌼 In Loving Memory of Faith 🌼

Forever loved. Forever remembered.

Faith was a gentle, loving soul who touched the lives of everyone around her.

She had a beautiful smile, a caring heart, and an incredible ability to make people feel seen, accepted, and loved.

Her compassion, warmth, and loving spirit made a lasting impact on her family, friends, and everyone fortunate enough to know her.

Through this scholarship, Faith's kindness and light will continue to live on by recognizing students who embody the same loving and compassionate spirit that made her so special.

🌼 About the Scholarship 🌼

The Faith Nicole Hancock Memorial Scholarship recognizes a student who reflects the beautiful qualities Faith embodied.

♡

Kindness

Treating others with love and compassion.

🌼

Empathy

Helping others feel seen and accepted.

☀

Perseverance

Continuing forward through life's challenges.

🕊️

Loving Spirit

Making a positive impact on those around them.
