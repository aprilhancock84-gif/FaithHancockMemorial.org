🌼 In Loving Memory of Faith 🌼

Forever loved. Forever remembered.

Faith was a gentle and loving soul who touched everyone around her.

She had a beautiful smile, a caring heart, and an incredible ability to make people feel accepted, valued, and loved.

Her kindness, compassion, and loving spirit continue to inspire everyone who knew her.

This scholarship was created so Faith's light may continue shining through future generations.
