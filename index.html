Her Beautiful Spirit

About Faith

♥

Faith was a gentle, loving, and amazing person who cared deeply for the people around her. Her kindness, warmth, compassion, and ability to make others feel loved left a lasting impression on everyone who knew her.

The Faith Nicole Hancock Memorial Scholarship was created to celebrate her life and continue the love she gave so freely by supporting a student who reflects those same beautiful qualities.

Continuing Her Legacy

Scholarship Information

♥
♥

Scholarship Amount

$1,000

One scholarship will be awarded to a deserving student who reflects the kindness, compassion, strength, and loving spirit Faith shared with others.

✉

How to Apply

Applicants should submit an email explaining why they believe they should receive the Faith Nicole Hancock Memorial Scholarship.

The message may include examples of kindness, compassion, perseverance, service to others, or the positive difference the applicant hopes to make.

Submit an Application

Please include the applicant's full name, school, phone number, and a personal message describing why they should receive the scholarship.

Apply by Email

Use “Faith Nicole Hancock Memorial Scholarship Application” as the email subject.
