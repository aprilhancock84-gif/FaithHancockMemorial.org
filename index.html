Faith Nicole Hancock Memorial Scholarship

Honoring a beautiful life filled with kindness, love, and compassion.

In Loving Memory of Faith Nicole Hancock

Faith was a gentle, loving soul who touched the lives of everyone around her. She had a beautiful smile, a caring heart, and an incredible ability to make people feel seen, accepted, and loved.

This scholarship was created in her memory to continue spreading the kindness, compassion, and light that she shared so freely throughout her life.

About the Scholarship

The Faith Nicole Hancock Memorial Scholarship will be awarded to a student who exemplifies the qualities Faith embodied:

  • Kindness toward others
  • Compassion and empathy
  • Perseverance through life's challenges
  • A loving and giving spirit
  • Making a positive impact on those around them

Additional scholarship information and application details will be announced soon.
