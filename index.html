Her Story

About Faith

🌼

Faith Nicole Hancock was deeply loved by her family and friends. She brought laughter, personality, and light into the lives of the people who knew her.

Her life continues to matter. Through this scholarship, shared memories, and community outreach, we hope to preserve the love she gave and use her story to create something meaningful for future generations.

Faith's legacy reminds us that one life can leave a lasting impact, and that love can continue to guide us even after loss.

“Please be the Faith that guides me.”

Creating Opportunity

The Scholarship

🕊️

The Faith Nicole Hancock Memorial Scholarship was created to help a student take a meaningful next step after high school. We believe success can take many forms, including college, technical education, trade training, certification programs, and practical job skills.

Who May Apply

Eligible students should be graduating seniors who are preparing to continue their education, enter a skilled trade, complete a certification, or pursue another clear path toward a stable future.

What We Value

We are looking for honesty, heart, perseverance, responsibility, and a genuine desire to grow. Applicants do not need to have a perfect record or a perfect plan.

More Than Tuition

Depending on available resources, support may include education expenses, job-skills guidance, mentorship, or practical help preparing for the next stage of life.

Your Story Matters

How to Apply

✉️

To apply, send an email telling us about yourself and why you believe you should receive the Faith Nicole Hancock Memorial Scholarship.

You may include your goals, challenges you have overcome, the direction you hope to take after graduation, and how this scholarship would help you move forward.

Please include:

Your full name, school, graduation year, contact information, intended education or career path, and your personal statement.

Giving Back to Our Community

Helping Young People Make Safe Choices

♥

The Faith Nicole Hancock Memorial Scholarship is about more than providing financial support. Our mission is also to help young people understand how the choices they make can affect their own lives, their friends, their families, and their entire community.

By sharing Faith's story, we hope to encourage honest conversations about impaired driving, distracted driving, peer pressure, and the importance of making responsible decisions before getting behind the wheel or riding with someone else.

School Presentations

Our family is available to speak with students at schools, churches, youth groups, and community events.

Practical Guidance

Presentations may include Faith's story, impaired-driving awareness, seat-belt safety, distracted-driving prevention, peer pressure, and safe-ride planning.

Courageous Choices

Asking for a safe ride, speaking up, putting away a phone, and refusing to ride with an impaired driver are acts of courage.

Invite Us to Speak

Schools and community organizations may contact us to discuss a safe-driving presentation, student event, awareness program, or another opportunity to serve local young people.
