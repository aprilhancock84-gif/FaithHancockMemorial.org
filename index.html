Her Beautiful Spirit

About Faith

♥

Faith was a gentle, loving, and amazing young woman who deeply cared for everyone around her. She had a beautiful ability to make others feel seen, loved, and important.

Though her time here was far too short, the impact she made on the lives of others will continue forever. This scholarship was created to celebrate her life and encourage others to live with the same compassion and kindness she shared so freely.

Scholarship Amount

$1,000

How To Apply

Applicants should submit an email describing:

  • How they have shown kindness to others
  • The impact they hope to make in the world
