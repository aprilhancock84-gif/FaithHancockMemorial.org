In Loving Memory of Faith

🌼
Forever loved. Forever remembered.

Faith was a gentle, loving soul who touched the lives of everyone around her. She had a beautiful smile, a caring heart, and an incredible ability to make people feel seen, accepted, and loved.

She carried kindness with her wherever she went. Her compassion, warmth, and loving spirit made a lasting impact on her family, friends, and everyone fortunate enough to know her.

This scholarship was created in Faith's memory so that her light can continue to shine through the lives of students who share her kindness, perseverance, and compassion for others.

🌼

About the Scholarship

🌼

The Faith Nicole Hancock Memorial Scholarship will recognize a student who reflects the beautiful qualities Faith embodied throughout her life.

♡

Kindness

Treating others with care, respect, and genuine compassion.

🌼

Compassion

Showing empathy and helping others feel valued and accepted.

☀

Perseverance

Continuing forward with courage through life's challenges.

🕊️

A Loving Spirit

Making a positive difference in the lives of people around them.
