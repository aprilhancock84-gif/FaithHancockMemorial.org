Giving Back

Safe Driving Awareness

♥

In memory of Faith, we hope to honor her life by encouraging young people to make safe choices and understand the lasting impact that impaired and distracted driving can have on families and communities.

We are available to speak with schools, churches, youth groups, and community organizations about safe driving, responsible decision-making, and the importance of protecting ourselves and others on the road.

Each presentation can be tailored to the age of the students and the needs of the school or organization.
