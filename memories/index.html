#1DForLife

Shared by Heaven Archer, Best Friend

Faith will forever be one of my very best friends. She was the kindest, sweetest, funniest soul I've ever known. She had the prettiest smile and a beautiful, creative spirit that touched everyone around her.

I could never choose just one memory because every moment with Faith was special. There was never a dull moment. We went through high school together, lived together twice—even once in an RV, just the two of us—and created countless memories filled with laughter and love.

Faith held my firstborn, sang to us, made us laugh during serious moments, and brought light into even the darkest days. She was a ray of sunshine and had an adorably contagious smile that I will never forget.

She still finds ways to remind me she is near through her favorite songs, always appearing at exactly the right moment. I carry her name, memories, and love with me every single day.

I pray her memory is never forgotten because there is not a day that goes by that I do not think about her.

"I love you Faith baby. Missing you booping my nose and seeing your gorgeous face forever. #1DForLife, tell Journey I said hello."

— Heaven Archer 🩷
